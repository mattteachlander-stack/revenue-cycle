**[Facility letterhead]**

**Letter of Intent — CORE pilot programme**

**Between:** [Facility legal name] (ABN [—]) ("the Facility")
**And:** Counterpart Health [entity TBC] ("Counterpart")
**Date:** [—]

1. **Purpose.** The Facility intends to participate in a 12-week pilot of the
   CORE platform (Contracting, Revenue Integrity and Enquiry suites), timed to
   the Facility's [fund name] agreement renewal expected [month/year].

2. **What the Facility will provide**, under the confidentiality deed at
   item 5: (a) its current [fund] hospital purchaser–provider agreement;
   (b) 12–24 months of de-identified billing/case-mix extracts; (c) summary
   financial information reasonably required for benchmarking; (d) 2–3 hours
   per fortnight of executive time and candid feedback.

3. **What Counterpart will provide:** (a) pilot access to CORE at no charge
   for the pilot term; (b) a clause-intelligence review and negotiation
   positioning paper for the live renewal; (c) drafted correspondence support
   through the negotiation rounds; (d) audit-response support through the
   Revenue Integrity workbench; (e) founding-customer pricing if the Facility
   continues after the pilot.

4. **Success measures** (assessed jointly at week 12): realised rate/terms
   movement versus the fund's opening position; executive assessment of
   output quality; Enquiry-suite answer accuracy on a question set drawn from
   the Facility's own agreement; executive time saved versus the last
   negotiation.

5. **Confidentiality & data handling.** All Facility data is handled under a
   mutual confidentiality deed [attach], with Australian data residency,
   per-facility isolation, no use of Facility data for any other customer,
   and deletion on request at pilot end.

6. **No obligation.** This letter records intent only; either party may
   withdraw on 14 days' notice. It creates no exclusivity, no fee obligation,
   and no licence beyond the pilot term.

**Signed:**
