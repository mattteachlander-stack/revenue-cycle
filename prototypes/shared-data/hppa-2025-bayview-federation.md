# Hospital Purchaser–Provider Agreement (extract)

**Federation Health Insurance Ltd** (ABN 00 000 000 003) ("**the Fund**")
and
**Bayview Day Surgery Pty Ltd** (ABN 00 000 000 002) ("**the Hospital**")

Commencement date: **1 July 2025** · Expiry: **30 June 2028**

> **SYNTHETIC DOCUMENT.** Fictional contract written for the prototype, as the
> *second* agreement in Bayview's portfolio. It is deliberately more favourable

### 1. Parties and purpose
1.1 This Agreement is a hospital purchaser–provider agreement for the purposes
of the *Private Health Insurance Act 2007* (Cth) covering Hospital Treatment
provided by the Hospital to Federation policyholders.

### 2. Term and renewal
2.1 Three-year term commencing 1 July 2025, expiring 30 June 2028.

2.2 If a replacement agreement is not concluded at expiry, this Agreement
continues month-to-month and **rates are indexed under clause 9 during any
holdover period.**

### 3. Payment structure
3.1 All Episodes are remunerated by bundled Case Payment per the Rate Schedule
(Schedule A), banded in five groups by procedure complexity.

3.2 **Listed Prostheses (including intraocular lenses) are payable at the
Prescribed List benefit in addition to the Case Payment.**

### 4. Claims and payment
4.1 Claims are submitted via ECLIPSE. **The Fund will pay clean claims within
14 days of receipt.**

4.2 **A claim must be lodged within 60 days of the date of discharge.** The
Fund will not reject a late claim where the delay arises from circumstances
beyond the Hospital's reasonable control.

4.3 Claim adjustments must be notified with grounds within 90 days of payment;
**the Fund may not recover an alleged overpayment by set-off more than 12
months after payment**, and amounts under dispute are excluded from set-off
until resolved.

### 5. Banding and classification
5.1 Procedures are assigned to bands in Schedule A. **Reassignment of a
procedure between bands requires the written agreement of both parties**;
failing agreement within 60 days, either party may refer the question to an
independent clinical coding expert whose determination binds both parties.

### 6. Audit
6.1 The Fund may audit claims on 15 business days' notice, no more than twice
per calendar year, limited to episodes within the preceding **12 months**.
Audit findings are subject to the dispute resolution process before any
recovery action.

### 7. Quality
7.1 The Hospital must maintain NSQHS accreditation and report the Schedule B
KPI set quarterly. **Sustained top-quartile performance against Schedule B
entitles the Hospital to a 0.5% quality loading on the next indexation event.**

### 8. Termination
8.1 **Either party may terminate this Agreement without cause on 60 days'
written notice.**

8.2 Either party may terminate for unremedied material breach (30 days) or
immediately on insolvency or loss of licence/accreditation.

### 9. Indexation
9.1 On each 1 July, rates in Schedule A are adjusted by **the full percentage
movement in CPI** (All Groups, weighted average of eight capital cities) over
the 12 months to the preceding March quarter. There is no discretionary
reduction mechanism. Movements above 5.0% trigger a good-faith review rather
than an automatic cap.

### 10. Confidentiality and disputes
10.1 Terms confidential on substantially the same basis as market practice;
CEO-level negotiation then mediation (Resolution Institute, Melbourne) before
proceedings.

### Comparison cheat-sheet (for the demo corpus — not part of the contract)

| Term | Federation (this agreement) | AusCare (2023 agreement) |
|---|---|---|
| Termination without cause | **60 days, either party** (cl. 8.1) | Fund 90 days / Hospital 180 days (cl. 19.1) |
| Payment of clean claims | **14 days** (cl. 4.1) | 15 business days (≈ 21 calendar) (cl. 14.1) |
| Claim lodgement window | **60 days**, reasonable-delay carve-out (cl. 4.2) | 30 days, hard bar (cl. 14.2) |
| Set-off / recovery | 12 months, disputes excluded (cl. 4.3) | 24 months, unrestricted (cl. 14.5) |
| Re-banding | Mutual consent → independent expert (cl. 5.1) | Fund unilateral, 30 days' notice (cl. 8.4) |
| Indexation | Full CPI, no carve-out (cl. 9.1) | CPI capped 4%, discretionary carve-out (cl. 12.3) |
| Holdover | Indexed (cl. 2.2) | Frozen (cl. 2.2) |
| IOL / prostheses | Paid separately at Prescribed List (cl. 3.2) | IOL bundled into cataract rate (cl. 10.2) |
| Quality | 0.5% loading for top-quartile KPIs (cl. 7.1) | KPI obligations, no upside (cl. 17.3) |
