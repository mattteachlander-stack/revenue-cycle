# CORE Build Extension Prompt (v2) — canonical copy

> Supplied by the founder. Extends `CLAUDE.md` (the original brief); does not
> replace it. All original architecture, modules and design philosophy remain
> in scope. The Negotiation Agent remains the flagship and market entry point.

## Direction

CORE becomes the **commercial intelligence layer between private hospitals and
private health insurers** — emulating the knowledge, reasoning and commercial
judgement of an experienced health fund contract executive. It researches,
analyses, benchmarks, models, recommends and explains; every recommendation
evidence-backed.

## New capability set (summarised from the full prompt)

1. **Negotiation Intelligence Engine** — continuously gathered intelligence:
   APRA financials, premium history, benefit/expense ratios, membership growth,
   capital adequacy, claims trends, market & local share, catchment/competitor
   data, capacity, announcements/annual reports/CEO commentary, regulatory
   change, news, historical negotiations & outcomes, internal + external
   benchmarks.
2. **Health fund intelligence profiles** — financial, commercial and
   negotiation-behaviour intelligence per fund.
3. **Hospital intelligence profiles** — catchment, demographics, service/DRG/
   payer/revenue mix, quality, capacity, competition, referral patterns,
   strengths/weaknesses.
4. **Dependency analysis** — hospital↔fund revenue/admissions dependency,
   alternatives, service uniqueness, market leverage; displayed visually.
5. **Negotiation Leverage Index** — proprietary composite score with full
   explanation: why, what raises/lowers it, recommended posture.
6. **Negotiation Workspace V2** — research → analyse fund → analyse hospital →
   benchmark → risks → opportunities → leverage → strategy → scenarios → board
   paper → exec brief → counter → letter → live support.
7. **Board-ready outputs** — CEO brief, board paper, negotiation pack, exec
   summary, risk & opportunity assessments, financial impact, strategy, counter
   proposal, talking points, risk register, implementation plan.
8. **Data-source integrations (design for)** — APRA, ABS, AIHW, IHACPA, DoH,
   ACCC, annual reports, media, internal contract DB, historical negotiations,
   uploads, activity/revenue/quality data.
9. **Reasoning standard** — why, evidence, assumptions, confidence,
   alternatives, commercial/operational impact, implementation considerations.
10. **Future agents** — Negotiation, Contract Intelligence, Contract
    Optimisation, Compliance, Revenue Integrity, Revenue Leakage, Audit
    Defence, Quality Intelligence, Casemix Intelligence, Forecasting,
    Executive Insights, Knowledge.
11. **Contract Clause Intelligence Engine** — classify every clause
    (commercial/financial/operational/compliance/reporting/clinical/audit/
    legal/quality/data/payment/termination/governance/risk); per clause:
    purpose, plain-English, impacts, burden, complexity, negotiation priority,
    risk rating. **Unfair clause identification** with reasons (one-sided
    obligations, unilateral variation, excessive audit, open liabilities,
    broad indemnities, short notice, penalties, payment delays, recovery
    provisions, ambiguity, off-market terms…).
12. **Clause Valuation Engine** — Method 1 automated (PAS/revenue/activity/
    claims/audit/DRG/payer/quality data); Method 2 rules-based estimate;
    Method 3 human-in-the-loop (annual value, negotiation value, strategic
    importance, confidence, rationale) with override + full audit trail.
13. **Negotiation Lever Register** — per contract: clause ref/title, issue,
    why it matters, value, burden, importance, ease, likelihood, recommended +
    fallback positions, dependencies, evidence.
14. **Negotiation Package Optimiser** — bundle levers; prioritise value,
    balance outcomes, tradeables vs must-haves, concession strategy, total
    opportunity, dependencies, quick wins, sequencing.
15. **Commercial Dashboard** — total opportunity, annual value, high-risk
    clauses, high-value levers, burden score, complexity score, clause risk
    heatmap, readiness score, top-ten priorities, change progress.
16. **Contract Change Intelligence Engine** — compare current vs proposed
    renewal, clause vs amendment, multiple drafts, hospital- and fund-proposed
    clauses; classify favourability; change impact analysis (14 dimensions);
    change valuation (automated + manual); **hospital-proposed clause
    development** (funding models, No-Gap, bundled care, quality incentives,
    audit reform, VBC…) with AI-drafted wording, rationale, impact, objections,
    strategy, alternatives; **negotiation scenario modelling** (accept/partial/
    reject/counter/concessions); **visual version comparison**; **negotiation
    change register** (originator, status, owner, priority, value, decisions,
    evidence, final position, implementation actions).

## Design principles

Evidence over opinion · commercial reasoning over summarisation ·
executive-ready outputs · board-quality documentation · Australian healthcare
terminology · transparency · source attribution · explainable AI · scalable
architecture.

## End goal

The definitive AI Commercial Intelligence Platform for Australian private
hospitals: the expertise, market intelligence, benchmarking and negotiation
capability of an experienced contracting team, for organisations that don't
have one.
